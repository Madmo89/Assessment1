Art

Art has always been a big part of my life. Thought I haven't had much time to do anything creative, I am hopeful that this new path I have started will finally allow me to get my creative juices flowing again.

Reading

A good book can take you to other worlds, to meet friends you never would have met in life. To find yourself on an adventure that the bounds of real life don't allow. Magic exists. It is Storytelling.

Writing

Going hand in hand with the others, writing is it's own form of art. The inspirations that come from art and reading push me to do to my own storytelling. Weaving words together to form a beautiful, though sometimes dark, painting that the reader paints inside their own head.
